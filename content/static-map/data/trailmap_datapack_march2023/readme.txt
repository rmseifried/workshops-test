
*******************
Trail Map Data Pack
*******************

This data pack contains geospatial files used to create a trail map of Erving State Forest, MA. It was compiled for the workshop "Design a Custom Map in QGIS" held virtually for UMass Amherst Libraries on March 21, 2023.

The following files are excerpts of MassGIS data, clipped to the extent of Erving State Forest. All data are in the NAD83 Massachusetts Mainland coordinate system (EPSG:26986):  

'dcr_roads_trails_arc_exerpt.shp' - Excerpt of MassGIS Data: Department of Conservation and Recreation Roads & Trails (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-department-of-conservation-and-recreation-roads-trails). Original file: DCR_ROADS_TRAILS_ARC.shp.

'dcr_roads_trails_pt_excerpt.shp' - Excerpt of MassGIS Data: Department of Conservation and Recreation Roads & Trails (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-department-of-conservation-and-recreation-roads-trails). Original file: DCR_ROADS_TRAILS_PT.shp.

'elevation_contours_excerpt.shp' - Excerpt of MassGIS Data: Elevation Contours (1:5,000) (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-elevation-contours-15000). Individual tiles were downloaded, merged, dissolved by contour (m), and then clipped to the extent of the Erving State Forest. Original files: hp91.shp, hp217.shp, hp223.shp, hp312.shp, hp319.shp.

'eotmajorroads_arc_excerpt.shp' - Excerpt of MassGIS Data: Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT) Roads (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-massachusetts-department-of-transportation-massdot-roads). Original file: EOTMAJROADS_ARC.shp.

'openspace_poly_excerpt.shp' - Excerpt of MassGIS Data: Protected and Recreational OpenSpace (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-protected-and-recreational-openspace). Original file: OPENSPACE_POLY.shp.

The following file is an excerpt of Natural Earth data, projected from WGS84 to the NAD83 Massachusetts Mainland coordinate system (EPSG:26986):  

'ne_10m_admin_1_ma.shp' - Excerpt of Natural Earth large-scale (1:10m) cultural data, admin level 1 (states and provinces) (https://www.naturalearthdata.com/downloads/10m-cultural-vectors/). Original file: ne_10m_admin_1_states_provinces.shp.
